**Data Insights for Optimizing Automobile Sales Strategies**
**A Data Cleaning, EDA, and Business Insights Project**
**Project Overview**
This project analyzes **an automobile sales dataset** to uncover patterns in customer demographics, vehicle preferences, spending behavior, and financial indicators. The goal is to generate actionable insights and business recommendations that can help automobile companies optimize their sales and marketing strategies.
The **analysis** includes:
- Data cleaning and preprocessing
- Exploratory data analysis (univariate & bivariate)
- Visual insights
- Answers to key business questions
- Actionable recommendations for decision‑makers

**Dataset Description**
The dataset contains detailed information about individuals and their vehicle purchases, including:
- Demographics: Age, Gender, Marital Status, Education
- Financials: Salary, Partner Salary, Total Household Salary
- Loans: Personal Loan, House Loan
- Family: Number of Dependents, Partner Working Status
- Vehicle Details: Price, Make (SUV/Sedan/Hatchback)
